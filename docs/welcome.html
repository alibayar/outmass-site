Your first campaign in 3 steps

OutMass lives inside Outlook on the web — here's how to find it and send your first personalized campaign.

1

Open Outlook on the web

OutMass works in the browser version of Outlook — not the Windows/Mac desktop app.

Open Outlook (work / school)

Personal @outlook.com or @hotmail.com account? Open outlook.live.com instead.

2

Click the round OM button

Look at the bottom-right corner of your Outlook — the round OutMass button opens the campaign panel on the right. (If you don't see it, refresh the Outlook tab once.)

3

Sign in with Microsoft, then send

Sign in first — sending uses your own Outlook account, so OutMass needs your permission. Then upload your recipients as a CSV (there's a template in the panel), write your email with {{firstName}} to personalize it, hit Preview or a Test Send, and send. OutMass paces delivery and tracks opens, clicks and replies.

💡 Your list comes from Excel? Save it as "CSV UTF-8 (Comma delimited)" — Excel's default "CSV" option can garble non-English characters.

Stuck or have a question? Email support@getoutmass.com — it goes straight to the founder, and replies usually come within a few hours.
