Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Pro features before buying?

Yes. Every new account gets a 7-day Pro trial with full access to all features.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards via Stripe. Subscriptions are billed monthly.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel from your account settings at any time. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes — we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. See our refund policy for details.

Do emails go through your servers?

No. Emails are sent directly through Microsoft Graph API using your own Outlook account. We never store your email content.
