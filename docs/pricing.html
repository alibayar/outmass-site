Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Pro features before buying?

You can start on Free and upgrade anytime. Cancel anytime — no long-term contract.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards via Stripe. Subscriptions are billed monthly.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel from your account settings at any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes — we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. See our refund policy.

Do emails go through your servers?

No. Emails are sent directly through Microsoft Graph API using your own Outlook account. We never store your email content.

What languages does OutMass support?

The extension UI, unsubscribe pages, and AI Writer all support 10 languages: English, Turkish, German, French, Spanish, Russian, Arabic (RTL), Hindi, Simplified Chinese, and Japanese.

What happens if I exceed my monthly email limit?

Sending pauses until next month, or you can upgrade your plan anytime.

⚠️ Important: Microsoft sending limits apply

OutMass sends emails through your own Microsoft Outlook account via the Graph API. Microsoft enforces its own sending limits on top of our plan quotas:

  • Outlook.com (personal): ~300 emails/day
  • Microsoft 365 Business: ~10,000 emails/day
  • Exchange Online: 30 messages/minute throttle

Our plan quotas (Free: 50, Starter: 2,000, Pro: 10,000 per month) apply in addition to these limits — whichever is stricter takes effect. We recommend spacing large campaigns over multiple days to avoid Microsoft rate limits and maintain sender reputation.

Microsoft may update these limits without notice. OutMass cannot override or guarantee delivery against Microsoft's platform limits.
